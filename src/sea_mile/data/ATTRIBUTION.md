# Bundled registry attribution

The bundled registry contains normalized records derived from:

- National Geospatial-Intelligence Agency, World Port Index (WPI). No copyright
  is claimed under Title 17 U.S.C. for the United States Government work.
- GeoNames geographical database, licensed under
  [Creative Commons Attribution 4.0](https://creativecommons.org/licenses/by/4.0/).
  This product contains normalized GeoNames data, available from
  <https://www.geonames.org/>.

The exact source URLs, snapshot labels, sizes, and SHA-256 digests are recorded
in `registry_manifest.json`.

UN/LOCODE and OpenStreetMap records are not included in the bundled registry.
They may be added to a locally built registry through the documented data build
workflow and remain subject to their respective terms.
